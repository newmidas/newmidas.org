### Doctoral education in the UK

-  The PhD experience is well-known to be extremely stressful.
-  Doctoral-level education in the UK is not available to many who would make excellent candidates.
-  Manually writing a thesis -- an entire _book_ -- in the age of <abbr title="artificial intelligence">AI</abbr> and <abbr title="large language models">LLMs</abbr> is an archaic approach.
-  The _viva voce_ examination is highly problematic.
-  PhD projects -- and over-arching doctoral programmes -- are typically formulated in advance and hence cannot respond to the fast-moving real world.
